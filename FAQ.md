# Frequently Asked Questions

## What is BeliefMarket?
A privacy-preserving prediction market powered by Fully Homomorphic Encryption.

## How does FHE work?
FHE allows computation on encrypted data without revealing it. Your vote weight stays private until market settlement.

## Which networks are supported?
Currently Ethereum Sepolia testnet. Mainnet planned for 2025.

## How do I get test ETH?
Use Sepolia faucets like https://sepoliafaucet.com

## Is my data private?
Yes! Vote weights are encrypted client-side and remain encrypted on-chain.

## Can I create my own markets?
Yes, through the Create Market page (admin function).
